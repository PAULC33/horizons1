About Us - Origins

In labs all across the world, exhausted lab technicians are wasting hours of time doing the menial tasks associated with the first stage in production of a new bacterial cell culture. Transcription must be triggered at the exact right moment (via the addition of the IPTG reagent) to ensure optimal protein expression for growth; too late and the cells will be stunted and small, too soon and we can get overexpression resulting in mutations or decreased functionality. Here at BactoView we were painfully aware of the need for change, so we have designed and created the BactoViewer! One machine to be placed in the incubator with the culture, that will take periodic readings of the optimal density of the sample, and will alert the user when the IPTG must be added.

Our Product - The BactoViewer.

The BactoViewer is comprised of a system of lasers, photodiodes and mirrors - held geometrically aligned by a durable outer casing of ABS+ and a neodynium magnet set up - all controlled by an arduino and connected to a laptop for simple bluetooth transfer of all collected data.
